Create a Dynamic Video Resume

Introducing EmployGraduates, an online career-building resource that uses video resumes to give employers a better feel for who your graduates really are and what they have to offer.

Our resource enables graduates to introduce themselves to employers through video introductions and online resumes and portfolio that feature their skills, strengths, passion, and value propositions. The Public Profile feature, a live link to candidates' video resumes, will enable graduates to send their video resume links to employers, recruiters, and job postings using Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter.

Our custom recording application includes a teleprompter and template scripts to ensure that new graduates communicate their messages perfectly and professionally. Along with the Public Profile, the resume includes an embedded QR code. When the resume is printed or sent as a PDF, the QR code can be read by any mobile device and provides a custom link to the graduate's public profile video resume.

Our comprehensive training resource modules will enable graduates to update their job search skills, learn how to create professional resumes, and hone their live interview skills. The training program was created by a career recruitment professional and award-winning video interview producer, both of whom have well over 20 years of industry experience.

Graduates can control their online video resumes and portfolio by email or share their Public Profiles and their video resume QR codes through Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter or other social media networks. They may also send their Public Profiles directly to job postings.
